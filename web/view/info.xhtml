RMS, Refugee Management System, is an organization dedicated to save lives,protecting rights and building a better future for Rohingya refugees. Our mission is to provide help to the refugees specially Rohingya People like :different medical issues; when, where and what type of relief they can get ; different types of health tips and accommodation from help desk. The system is used to maintain the “Rohingya Refugee Database Management” to make the process easier.Moreover,it contains the database and stores the following information: People will get the current update of rohingya issues in the news update section. People who sign up as a donor will get a profile and will be able to donate the amount he want. It also includes doctor code, name, address and phone number. This information may be used for keeping the records of the doctor for any emergency or for any other kind of information. People who wants to take part as volunteer will be registered and their details information will be kept.
